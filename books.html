Summary

This book will serve as a continuation and an expansion of the work presented in the previous book. Building on the foundation laid in that previous work, the authors will delve deeper into the intricacies of Discrete Choice Experiments with a focus on practical application and methodological detail. The goal is to equip the reader with hands-on tools and resources to conduct their own analyses. This knowledge is informed by our collective experience as a group of academics who have collaborated closely over the past decade within the ENVECHO network.
