Summer schools

Envecho Group organised the following Summer Schools


1) Using Discrete Choice Experiments for the Economic Valuation of Ecosystem Services in Rural Landscapes

Organised by the Technical University of Berlin and the Institute for Ecological Research, in collaboration with the Leibniz Centre for Agricultural Landscape Research (ZALF) in Vierraden, Germany. This summer school took place from September 3 to September 9, 2017.

Instructors:

  • Katrin Rehdanz - Christian-Albrechts-Universität zu Kiel
  • Jürgen Meyerhoff - Berlin School of Economics and Law
  • Petr Mariel - University of the Basque Country, Bilbao

2) Valuing Options of Adaptation to Climate Change Using Choice Experiments

Organized by the School of Economics, University of Cape Town, this course was held at the University of Cape Town, South Africa, from August 29 to September 2, 2022.

Instructors:

  • Petr Mariel - University of the Basque Country, Bilbao
  • Jürgen Meyerhoff - Berlin School of Economics and Law
  • Djiby Thiam - University of Cape Town
  • Mark Zuidgeest - University of Cape Town

3) UEH-CELG Summer School 2024: Environmental Valuation using Discrete Choice Experiments - A Strategy for Sustainable Development in Southeast Asia

Further information at the summer school site

Organized by the University of Economics Ho Chi Minh City, College of Economics, Law and Government, this summer school will take place in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, from June 10 to June 14, 2024.

Instructors:

  • Petr Mariel - University of the Basque Country, Bilbao
  • Jürgen Meyerhoff - Berlin School of Economics and Law
  • Sven Anders - University of Alberta, Canada

4) Spring School on Environmental Valuation using Discrete Choice Experiment

Organised by Università degli Studi di Messina, Italy from May 26 to May 30, 2025.

Instructors:

  • Petr Mariel - University of the Basque Country, Bilbao
  • Jürgen Meyerhoff - Berlin School of Economics and Law
  • Maria De Salvo - Università degli Studi di Messina
